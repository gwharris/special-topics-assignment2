We help startups start up.

Our mission is to create a diverse, inclusive culture that facilitates & celebrates entrepreneurship for everyone in NYU's community as they create scalable solutions to meaningful problems.

By guiding entrepreneurs at every state in their journey, we are at the center of a startup ecosystem designed to transform brilliant ideas into successful commercial solutions. Since the founding of the NYU Innovation Venture Fund in 2010, we have accelerated the pace of technology commercialization and helped launch successful startups founded by our talented community of students, faculty, and researchers.
